Our Services

At Reigate Motor Company our many years of experience, the latest technology and Bosch Car Service status means that we can deal with all your needs, caring for the modern, technically complex vehicles of today. Our team of qualified technicians undertakes continuous Bosch training and this ensures we are always up-to-date with new technology and enables us to provide accurate diagnosis, service and repairs on all makes and model of car and light commercial vehicle. Our specialist diagnostic services are carried out using state-of-the-art equipment by Bosch and we use only the highest quality parts and oils. No job is too big or too small.

The Bosch Car Service network works to a Bosch Car Service Code of Practice. The Consumer Codes Approval Scheme, run by the Trading Standards Institute, has approved the Bosch Car Service Code of Practice. This commitment to customer care gives you peace of mind in knowing that you are going to be given not only the best technical service, but also the best customer service by Reigate Motor Company.
